Recipe

Garlic Shrimp Bowl

Low-carb, high-protein, and ready fast.

15 min 2 servings Fuel 1: Real Meal

Ingredients

  • 1 lb shrimp
  • 2 cups broccoli
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Garlic, lemon, salt

Steps

  1. Saute garlic and shrimp.
  2. Add veggies and cook until crisp.
  3. Finish with lemon and serve.

Why it works

  • High protein keeps you full.
  • Simple ingredients mean repeatability.
  • Fuel 1 sets the tone for the day.

Pair it with

  • Snack: yogurt + berries
  • Protein dessert: chocolate pudding

Make it more filling

  • Add avocado or extra veggies.
  • Serve with a protein dessert.
